SET TIMER:
3B 06 80 40 TT HH MM CK

TT = Timer 1(0F), Timer 2(10)
HH = Hour (24h)
MM = Minute
CK = Checksum

Timer 1 Switch:
ON  = 3B 05 80 41 0F 04 F4
OFF = 3B 05 80 41 0F 08 F8

Timer 2 Switch:
ON  = 3B 05 80 41 10 04 EB
OFF = 3B 05 80 41 10 08 E7


80 E7 2A 00 00 Aux_Heating_LED = Off
80 E7 2A 00 04 Aux_Heating_LED = On
80 E7 2A 00 08 Aux_Heating_LED = Flash
